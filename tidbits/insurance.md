## About
Insurance is given by default to help new players retain a little bit of their inventory on death.

## Coverage
The following is the amount of money you get back after the value of the items you took in with you.

| Difficulty   | % Returned |
|--------------|------------|
| Amateur      | 50%        |
| Intermediate | 25%        |
| Professional | 0%         |
